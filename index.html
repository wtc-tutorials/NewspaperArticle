Breaking: Cat Discovers Renewable Energy Source

Neighborhood Feline Accidentally Solves Global Power Crisis

By Alex Beam

In a shocking development for both scientists and local pet owners, a house cat named Mr. Whiskers has reportedly discovered a new form of renewable energy — by knocking over a fishbowl. The chain reaction that followed somehow powered every appliance on the block for eight minutes straight.

Experts are still baffled, describing the breakthrough as “scientifically impossible but undeniably adorable.” Meanwhile, Mr. Whiskers has shown no interest in giving interviews, preferring to nap on top of the now-famous fishbowl. Residents are calling him “the purr-fect genius” who just might save the planet—between naps, of course.
